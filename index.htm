About Me

      Hello there!! My name is Nur Arina Nadirah but you can call me Arin. I am a 20 years old and currently finishing up my studies as a part 5 student in UiTM Rembau, taking Diploma in Information Management.
      I currently live in Kuala Lumpur with my mom and dad. I was born in Taiping, Perak. However, my hometown is in Kuala Kangsar. I like staying in the countryside as it is more calm and peaceful compared to the city.
      Some of the things I enjoy doing are spending time with family and friends, listening to music, going out, shopping, baking and receiving flowers. I show my love by giving gifts and spending quality time. My fears include being alone, losing the people I love and the ocean. I have basic skills in playing the piano and swimming. I love learning new things, growing as a person and making new friends.
